## Top Questions Urban Commuters Ask About Cycling
1. "Will I reach office sweaty?" → Pedal-assist means minimal effort. 25 km/h, no sweat.
2. "Is 25 km/h too slow?" → Bangalore average traffic speed is 18 km/h. You'll be faster than cars.
3. "What about rain?" → Water-resistant for light rain. Bangalore has ~6 months dry weather.
4. "Where do I charge?" → Regular plug point. 3-4 hour charge. Sherpa M1 battery is removable — charge at your desk.
5. "Is it safe on Bangalore roads?" → Hydraulic disc brakes, fat tires, dual suspension (Street Mamba). Built for Indian roads.
